MARS

Mars is thefourth planet and the furthest terrestrial planet from the Sun. The reddish color of its surface is due to finely grained iron(III) oxide dust in the soil, giving it the nickname "the Red Planet".

ROTATION

Radio-wave data from NASA's InSight mission has provided the most precise measurement of Mars' rotation rate ever made. The length of Mars' day is shortening by three-quarters of a millisecond each year as the planet's rotation spins up, according to new results from NASA's InSight lander.

ORBIT

Mars has an orbit with a semimajor axis of1.524astronomical units (228 million km), and an eccentricity of 0.0934. The planet orbits the Sun in 687 days and travels 9.55 AU in doing so, making the average orbital speed 24 km/s.
